# Code of Conduct

The Hyperlocal Intent Broadcast Protocol (HIBP) community is committed to creating a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative environment for all contributors, implementers, and ecosystem participants.

---

## 🌐 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All GitHub repositories under the HIBP project  
- Issues, pull requests, and discussions  
- Community forums and public communication channels  
- Documentation and specification contributions  
- Any official HIBP events or virtual meetings  
- Interactions involving HIBP maintainers, contributors, or implementers  

---

## 🌱 Our Standards

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive  
- Use welcoming and inclusive language  
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully  
- Focus on what is best for the community and the protocol  
- Show empathy toward others  
- Collaborate in good faith  
- Follow the versioning and governance rules of the HIBP project  

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or derogatory comments  
- Personal attacks or threats  
- Trolling, insulting, or inflammatory remarks  
- Publishing private or confidential information  
- Disruptive or intentionally unproductive behavior  
- Attempting to bypass governance or versioning rules  

---

## 🛠 Maintainer Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior  
- Reviewing and addressing reported violations  
- Taking appropriate and proportional corrective action  
- Applying this Code of Conduct consistently and fairly  
- Protecting the integrity of the HIBP project and community  

---

## ⚖️ Enforcement

Maintainers may take the following actions:

- Request modification or removal of comments  
- Issue a private or public warning  
- Temporarily restrict participation  
- Permanently ban contributors who violate this Code of Conduct  
- Close issues or pull requests that violate community standards  

All enforcement decisions will be made in the best interest of the HIBP community.

---

## 📬 Reporting Issues

To report violations, please contact the maintainers through Information Network Website using the designated contact page.

All reports will be handled confidentially.  
Retaliation against reporters is strictly prohibited.

---

## 🤝 Commitment

By participating in the HIBP project, you agree to uphold this Code of Conduct and contribute to a positive, collaborative, and respectful community.
